Rules & Moderation

Everyone can submit anonymously, but nothing appears on the wall unless the admin approves it. Keep it fun and safe for everyone.

1. Anonymous submissions

Anyone who visits the site can submit a confession without logging in.

2. Admin approval first

Every submission is stored with approved = false. After approval, it becomes visible on the wall.

3. Do not post

  • Real names, phone numbers, addresses
  • Hate speech, doxxing, threats
  • Anything that could harm someone

4. Length

We currently use a 240 character limit. Short, readable confessions make the wall look better.

5. Admin can decline

Off-topic, spammy or risky confessions may be rejected without explanation.

6. Future: token-only content

Later we may show extra confessions only to token holders. This page will be updated when that happens.
