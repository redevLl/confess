Privacy & Safety

This project is built to be anonymous first. We only keep what we need to review and show confessions.

What we store

We store the confession text you submit and the timestamp (created_at). We also store whether it was approved (approved).

What we show

Only rows from the database where approved = true are shown on the public wall.

What we don’t want

Please don’t submit real names, personal addresses, doxxing info, or anything that could be used to harm someone. Such posts will be rejected.

Why admin approval?

Anonymous walls get spammed fast. Manual approval keeps the feed fun, readable and safe for a global audience.
